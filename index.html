Your RR Dental, ID Dentists

Our dentists in RR Dental Care set the standard for high-quality care that is both comfortable and safe. We pride ourselves on our overwhelming number of positive reviews and successful patient experiences.

Whether we are performing general dentistry, or one of our specialty services, we approach our dentistry with an unmatched level of precision, care, and skill. That is why so many first-time visitors become regulars here at Taylorview Dental.

General Dentistry

Our general dentistry includes six different services that improve your smile and leave you feeling more confident about the look and feel of your teeth.

We treat our general dentistry work and first time visitors with an extreme amount of care whether we are performing work on, teeth cleaning, fillings, crowns, bridges, root canals, or dentures.

Dental Implants

We provide single-tooth replacement that can last more than 25 years.
Once complete, you will be able to chew, smile, and brush your teeth
the same as you did before.

There are many benefits from dental implants outside of the tooth replacement. For example you might actually experience stimulated bone growth around the implant, making the bone stronger.

Cosmetics

Our mission with cosmetics is to leave you with an extremely natural looking smile. Our doctors are specially-trained and certified to perform a number of different cosmetic services.

The long-lasting effects of our cosmetic dentistry can have such effects as improved chewing or even speaking, and in some cases our results are often in a single visit.

Oral Surgery

We provide several specialty services that affect the jaw or mouth area. One indicator that you may be in need of oral surgery is if you are experiencing a great deal of pain.

If you are experiencing pain from a decayed, diseased, or damaged tooth it may need to be extracted by performing oral surgery. We also treat temporomandibular joint disorder (TMD or TMJ) - which is one of the most common reasons for experiencing jaw pain and inflammation.
